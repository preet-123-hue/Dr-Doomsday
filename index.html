About the Movie

Dr. Doomsday combines the deadly powers of Doctor Doom and the monstrous Doomsday, creating a formidable and unstoppable threat to both Earth and the entire Marvel Universe. When the villain breaks free from his containment, he goes on a destructive rampage, forcing Earth's mightiest heroes to join forces and stop the catastrophic destruction he leaves behind.

With mind-bending action, epic battles, and a thrilling plot, "Dr. Doomsday: The Rise of Doom" promises to be one of the most intense Marvel films to date.
